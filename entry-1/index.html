Mini Chocolate Chip (Approx. 20 servings) Recipe
<Ingredients>
Unsalted butter 60g
Brown sugar 30g
Granulated sugar 75g
Half an egg
Vanilla extract 0.3 teaspoon
Salt 0.5g
All-purpose flour 110g
Baking powder 1
Dark chocolate 100g
<Steps to Make>
1. Combine sugar and pre-melted butter in a bowl with half an egg and mix well.
2. Add vanilla extract and mix thoroughly.
3. Sift all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt into the mixture, then stir until combined.
4. Finally, add the chocolate chips and mix until well incorporated.
5. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour to allow it to mature.
