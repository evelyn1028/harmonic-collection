Making Gimbap

prev next

Ingredients
Rice, Gim (seaweed for gimbap), Ham, Fish cake, Eggs, Carrots, Pickled radish
<Steps to Make>
1. In a bowl, beat 5 eggs and add a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well.
2. Slice the green onions into small pieces to remove the egg's raw taste.
3. Heat a frying pan with cooking oil and pour the beaten eggs to cook.
4. Grill the carrots over high heat.
5. Grill the fish cake and ham.
6. Now, all the gimbap ingredients are ready.
7. Spread the rice thinly and evenly on the gim (seaweed).
8. Place the prepared ingredients on top of the rice.
9. Cut the gimbap into bite-sized pieces.
