Healthy Habits

<Balanced diet>

Dairy
Milk is essential for both health and muscle development as it provide a rich source of calcium and protein.
Fruits and vegetables
Fruits and vegetables provide a wide range of nutrients such as minerals, vitamins, dietary fiber, antioxidants, strengthening the immune system and protecting the body. They also reduce the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, obesity, and diabetes, and improve digestion by enhancing digestive function. For these reasons, fruits and vegetables play a crucial role in a balanced diet.
Carbohydrates
Carbohydrates are essential in a balanced diet because they provide energy, support brain funciton, promote digestive health, enhance exercise performance, and help regulate bodily functions.
Protein
In a balanced diet, protein is crucial for muscle development, tissue repair, and maintaining a healthy immune system. It also aids in providing energy and satiety.
